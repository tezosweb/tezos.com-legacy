 ## 2. Write a Smart Contract
 Write smart contracts in your choice of syntax. Code will compile to Michelson, the smart contract language of the Tezos blockchain.
 - **Python** | <ExternalLink text="SmartPy" link="http://smartpy.io/" />
 - **Pascal, OCaml, ReasonML** | <ExternalLink text="LIGO" link="https://ligolang.org/" />
 - **Haskell** | <ExternalLink text="Indigo" link="https://indigo-lang.gitlab.io/" />
 - **Domain-Specific** | <ExternalLink text="Archetype" link="https://archetype-lang.org/" />
 - **Language of Tezos Smart Contracts** | <ExternalLink text="Michelson" link="https://michelson.nomadic-labs.com/" />
